A Celonis Process Mining course is a training program that teaches individuals how to use the Celonis Process Mining software and apply its capabilities to real-world business scenarios. Celonis Process Mining is a software tool that allows organizations to analyze and optimize their business processes by using data from their digital systems. It helps organizations to understand how their processes are actually being executed, identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies, and find ways to improve process efficiency and effectiveness.
Soft Computing.

Soft computing is a field of computer science that deals with the design and development of systems and algorithms that can solve complex problems that are difficult or impossible to solve using traditional methods of computation. It involves the use of techniques such as artificial neural networks, fuzzy logic, genetic algorithms, and machine learning to solve problems that are too complex or uncertain to be solved using classical methods.
